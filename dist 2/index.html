Swan Wharf Residency

GIANT STEPS has now taken residence at Swan Wharf, a Victorian warehouse complex in Hackney Wick. The space features a canal-side terrace and an indoor waterfront bar & event space where the GIANT STEPS sound system has been installed.

Food

Our resident food provider is Dhaba. Dhaba marries regional Indian cuisine with fantastic British produce and ingredients. Expect fiery Rajasthani stews, fluffy Biriyanis, butter rich Punjabi food, and tandoor grilled meats. The seasonality of British vegetables and meats informs regular menu changes and provides unlimited inspiration for new ways to look at traditional Indian cuisine.
